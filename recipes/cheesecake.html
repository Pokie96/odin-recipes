Cheesecake Recipe

Description

An easy no-cook cheesecake - a perfect and delicious summer dessert for all occasions!

Ingredients:

  • 250g digestive biscuits
  • 100g butter, melted
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 600g full fat soft cheese
  • 100g icing sugar
  • 284ml pot of double cream
  • 400g punnet of strawberries, halved (for topping)
  • 25g icing sugar (for topping)

Steps:

  1. Butter and line a loose-bottomed tin with baking parchment.
  2. Put crushed digestive biscuits in to a bowl with melted butter and mix until crumbs are completely coated.
  3. Transfer mix to the prepared tin and press down in to the base to create an even layer. Leave this in fridge for 1 hour to set.
  4. Slice the vanilla pod lengthways, leaving the tip intact so the two halves are still joined. Scrape out the seeds of the pod using the back of a kitchen knife.
  5. Pour the double cream into a bowl and whisk with a mixer until it's just starting to thicken.
  6. Place the soft cheese, icing sugar and vanilla seeds in seperate bowl, and beat with electric mixer until it starts to thicken.
  7. Add the double cream to the mixture and fold until mixed thoroughly and thick consistency.
  8. Spoon mixture on to the biscuit base, then smooth the top of the cheesecake.
  9. Leave the mix to set in fridge overnight.
  10. Bring cheesecake to room temp before serving.
  11. Purée half of the strawberries in a blender with the icing sugar and add the purée over the top.
  12. Add the remaining strawberries on top of the cake to serve
