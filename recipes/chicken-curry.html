Chicken Curry Recipe

Description:

An easy chicken curry recipe which is a fantastic choice for family dinners. Made with a handful of easy to use ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 6 chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 3 tbsp medium spice paste
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 100g Greek yogurt
  • 1 small bunch of coriander, leaves chopped
  • 50g ground almonds
  • Naan breads or cooked basmati rice, to serve

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in large frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and fry for 8-10 mins.
  3. Add garlic and ginger then cook for a further minute.
  4. Chop chicken into chunky pieces and add to the pan to fry for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir the spice paste and chopped tomatoes through the pan, along with 250ml water.
  6. Once brought to a boil, lower to a simmer and cook on gentle heat for 25-30 minutes.
  7. Then stir through the yoghurt, coriander and ground almonds.
  8. Serve with warm naan bread, fluffy basmati rice or both!
