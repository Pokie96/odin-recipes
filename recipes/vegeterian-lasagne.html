Vegeterian Lasagne Recipe

Description

An easy vegeterian lasagne that uses only a handful of ingredients. A tasty option for the whole family.

Ingredients:

  • 3 red peppers, cut into large chunks
  • 2 aubergines, cut into ½ cm thick slices
  • 8 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for the dish
  • 300g lasagne sheets
  • 125g mozzarella
  • Handful cherry tomatoes, halved

For tomato sauce:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 carrot, roughly chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 200ml white wine
  • 3 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of basil, leaves picked

For white sauce:

  • 85g butter
  • 85g plain flour
  • 750ml milk

Steps:

To make tomato sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan, adding onions, garlic and carrot and cook for 5-7 minutes over medium heat.
  2. Slightly turn up heat, stir in tomato purée and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the white wine then cook for a further 5 minutes until the mixture has reduced.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and the basil leaves to the sauce.
  5. Bring the sauce to the boil and then let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Once cool can be whizzed in food processor.

To make white sauce:

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan, stir the plain flour in, then cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Slowly whisk in the milk, and stir while bringing to the boil.
  3. Turn down the heat and cook until the sauce starts to thicken.

Putting it all together:

  1. Heat your oven to 200C/ 180C fan/ gas 6.
  2. Lightly oil two baking trays and add the peppers and aubergines.
  3. Season and coat with olive oil, then roast for 25 minutes.
  4. Reduce the oven to 180C/ 160C fan/ gas 4.
  5. Lightly oil an ovenproof dish and arrange a layer of the vegetables on the bottom, pour a third of the tomato sauce over, then top with a layer of lasagne sheets and a quarter of white sauce.
  6. Repeat the above step until you have three layers of pasta.
  7. Spoon any remaining white sauce over the pasta, ensuring the whole surface is covered and scatter mozzarella and cherry tomatoes over the top.
  8. Now bake this for 45 minutes until it is bubbling and golden.
